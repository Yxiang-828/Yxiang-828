## About

I am a **Computer Engineering + AI Minor student at NUS** building practical systems across AI agents, robotics, local-first software, and hardware-facing workflows.

Right now I am a **Robotics Software Intern at KABAM Robotics**, working on the Matrix-4 autonomous delivery robot across **ROS 2, MQTT, Docker, Jetson Orin AGX, SAM3, Nav2, C++ and Python**. I also teach **CS2113 Software Engineering & OOP** at NUS.

My bias is toward systems that survive real constraints: local inference, latency, hardware boundaries, messy inputs, deployment, and user workflows.
